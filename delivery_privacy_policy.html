Privacy Policy (Delivery Partner)

Effective Date: June 05, 2026

Welcome to the PickBazaar Delivery mobile application. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal data of our delivery partners ("Riders", "Agents", or "You").

This policy is strictly designed to comply with Google Play Store's Data Safety guidelines, particularly concerning Background Location Data.

1. Background Location Data (Prominent Disclosure)

PickBazaar Delivery collects location data to enable order assignments and provide live order tracking for customers and sellers even when the app is closed or not in use.

Why we need this: As a delivery partner, your precise location is essential for our dispatch system to assign you the nearest available orders. Furthermore, once you accept an order, your live location is shared with the customer so they can track their delivery in real-time. This tracking continues in the background even if you switch to another app (like Google Maps) to navigate.

How to stop sharing: You can stop sharing your location at any time by toggling your status to "Offline" within the app. We do not track your location while your status is Offline.

2. Other Information We Collect

A. Personal and Identity Data:

  • Contact Details: Name, phone number, and email address.
  • Identity & Vehicle Documents: Driver's license, vehicle registration, and ID proofs required for legally mandated background checks and onboarding.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details used strictly to process your delivery earnings and payouts.

B. Device Permissions We Request:

  • Location (Foreground & Background): Essential for order dispatching and live customer tracking as detailed above.
  • Camera: To take your profile picture, capture photos of identity documents during onboarding, and take "Proof of Delivery" photos when handing over packages.
  • Storage (Read/Write): To upload existing documents from your device gallery and cache map data.
  • Phone / Call Logs: To allow you to seamlessly call the customer or seller directly from the app regarding an active order.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and legal eligibility to work as a delivery partner.
  • To dynamically calculate your delivery distances, routes, and corresponding payouts.
  • To share your name, phone number, and live location with the customer and seller only during an active delivery task.
  • To provide you with in-app support and resolve any delivery disputes (using your location history and Proof of Delivery photos).

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information. We only share necessary details (name, phone number, and live location) with the specific customer and seller involved in your current delivery task. Your financial and identity documents are stored in securely encrypted cloud buckets (e.g., Google Firebase) and are only accessible by authorized PickBazaar administrators.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your profile data and transaction history for as long as you are an active partner, and thereafter as required by tax and labor laws. Your high-frequency location data is only retained for a short period to resolve immediate customer disputes.

How to Request Data Deletion:

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal data.

  • Inside the App: Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • Via Email: Send a request to delivery-support@pickbazaar.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request and delete your non-legally-binding data within 7-14 business days.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your location data is handled, please contact:
