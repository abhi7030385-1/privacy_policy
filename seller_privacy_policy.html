Privacy Policy (Seller Partner)

Effective Date: June 05, 2026

Welcome to the PickBazaar Seller application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data of our merchant partners and store owners.

This policy is designed to comply with Google Play Store requirements regarding the collection of business, financial, and personal data.

1. Information We Collect

A. Business and Personal Data:

  • Store Information: Store name, description, business address, and store logos/banners.
  • Contact Details: Owner's name, email address, and phone number for account management.
  • Legal & Identity Data: Business registration documents, Tax IDs (e.g., GSTIN), and owner identity proofs required for legal compliance and vendor verification.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details or UPI IDs required exclusively to process your sales payouts and settlements.
  • Product Data: Images, videos (Reels), descriptions, and pricing data of the products you upload to the platform.

B. Device Permissions We Request:

  • Camera: To allow you to take live photos of your products, scan barcodes, and record video reels to promote your store directly from the app.
  • Storage (Read/Write): To allow you to upload existing product images, documents, and videos from your device gallery.
  • Internet: To sync your product inventory, receive real-time order notifications, and process customer interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Manage Your Storefront: Your store name, address, and product catalog are made publicly visible on the PickBazaar Customer App so users can shop from you.
  • Order Fulfillment: We share your store's physical location with our Delivery Partners so they can navigate to your store to pick up customer orders.
  • Payouts: Your financial data is securely used to transfer your earnings on a scheduled basis.
  • Compliance: Your identity and tax documents are used strictly to comply with local e-commerce and tax laws.

3. Data Visibility and Sharing

Public Visibility: By nature of being a merchant on our platform, your store name, public address, product listings, and store ratings are visible to all users of the PickBazaar platform.

Third-Party Processors: We share your financial settlement data with secure, certified payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay) to process your payouts. Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (e.g., Google Firebase).

4. Data Security

We treat your business and financial data with the highest level of security. Bank account details and sensitive KYC documents are encrypted both in transit and at rest. Access to this data is strictly limited to authorized PickBazaar compliance personnel.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your business data as long as you maintain an active seller account with us. Due to financial regulations and tax compliance, certain transaction records must be retained for a legally mandated period even after account closure.

How to Request Account Deletion:

As per Google Play Store guidelines, you can request the deletion of your account and eligible data.

  • Inside the App: Navigate to Profile > Settings > Close Store / Delete Account.
  • Via Email: Send an email to sellers@pickbazaar.com with the subject "Store Deletion Request". We will initiate the delisting of your products and the deletion of your personal profile, retaining only what is legally required for accounting purposes.
